Events

Cool events all year round!

VR and Chill

Immerse yourself into VR on our Oculus Rifts and Gos, play games like Beat Saber, Superhot VR, and some amazing board games! The event is open to everyone for FREE and we'll have some tasty snacks! .

VR/AR Workshops

Make your way to our Introduction to XR workshops, learn about the industry and build a project using tools like Unity, ARCore, and Vuforia!

Other

We are always working on designing new events. If you have any ideas for events, feel free to email us, or even apply to be on the exec team!
