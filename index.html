About

UW Virtual Reality is Canada’s first university VR/AR club. We are dedicated to increasing awareness about careers in the field, encouraging project development among students, and hosting cool events around new experiences in VR.

Events

VR and Chill

Come join us and play some games. We'll provide all the equipment you need.

Workshops

Come and learn how to build games using Unity. People of all coding backgrounds are welcome.

Other

We are always working on designing new events. If you have any ideas for events you think we should host, feel free to email us!
